maq_fastq2bfq
------------------------------------------------

**Convert FASTQ to BFQ**


    Converts a FASTQ file to MAQ BFQ format.



Commands
~~~~~~~~

**clean**
  Remove all job data, not the Moa job itself, note that this must be implemented by the template.
  
  
**run**
  *no help defined*
  
  

Filesets
~~~~~~~~


**bfq**
  


  | *type*: `map`
  | *source*: `input`
  | *category*: `output`
  | *optional*: `{}`
  | *pattern*: `./bfq/*.bfq`




**input**
  input FASTA files
